## Responsive Breakpoints

### Mobile (< 640px)
- 5 cards visible at once
- Smaller gap (8px)
- Hidden scrollbar
- Touch-optimized

### Tablet (640px - 1024px)
- 7-8 cards visible
- Medium gap (12px)
- Visible scrollbar
- Hover enabled

### Desktop (> 1024px)
- 10-12 cards visible
- Full gap (12px)
- Visible scrollbar
- Full hover effects
